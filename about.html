About AzothSolver

Built for performance. Driven by architecture.

AzothSolver is an experimental, latency-optimized solver designed for the CoW Protocol auction environment. It targets sub-5 millisecond response times and is architected from scratch in Rust with zero reliance on Python tooling or third-party orchestration layers.

The solver is fully self-hosted, tuned for bare-metal performance, and intended for long-term use as a refiller, relayer, or autonomous solver candidate. AzothSolver does not rely on off-the-shelf abstractions — everything is built in-house for vertical control.

Our approach enables superior price discovery through faster batch optimization, reduced MEV leakage via optimized routing algorithms, and enhanced trader protection through improved surplus generation for CoW Protocol users.

CoW DAO Grant Vision

AzothSolver is a self-funded, high-performance solver in active development, engineered to achieve sub-5ms response times in CoW Protocol auctions. I'm committed to aligning its capabilities with CoW DAO's goals, with plans to pursue a grant to accelerate ecosystem impact once key performance benchmarks are validated.

Potential grant funding would fuel targeted contributions, such as open-source diagnostics tools for solver performance, detailed benchmarking reports to enhance protocol transparency, and selective open-sourcing of non-core components to benefit the CoW community.

To maintain competitive edge and explore future models like solver-as-a-service or zero-knowledge proving systems, the solver's core will remain closed-source for now. My focus is on delivering measurable value to CoW DAO through performance, reliability, and community-driven tools.
